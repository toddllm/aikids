About AI Kids Academy

Our Mission

AI Kids Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to making artificial intelligence education accessible, engaging, and fun for children. We believe that understanding AI is crucial for the next generation, and we're committed to providing high-quality, age-appropriate education in this field.

What We Do

We provide innovative educational programs that introduce children to the basics of artificial intelligence through:

  • Interactive workshops and classes
  • Online learning resources
  • Hands-on projects and experiments
  • Community events and workshops
  • Parent education programs
